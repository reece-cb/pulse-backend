## App Store Description (full)
Every day at 9pm, Pulse asks you 5 simple questions about your day.

In 60 seconds, our AI gives you a score out of 100 — and shows you exactly how you ranked against everyone else on the planet.

Then you share it.

📊 DAILY SCORE
Answer 5 quick questions about sleep, mood, productivity, money, and your one win. Get a personalized AI score and insight.

🌍 GLOBAL LEADERBOARD  
See how your day stacks up against the world. Did you win today?

📈 TRACK YOUR LIFE
30-day history. Calendar heatmap. Score trends. Watch yourself improve over time.

👥 COMPETE WITH FRIENDS
Share your daily Pulse card to your story. Challenge your friends to beat your score.

🤖 AI COACHING (Premium)
Get personalized insights about your patterns. Why are your Mondays always low? Pulse knows.

Pulse is the game you play with your own life.
